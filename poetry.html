Romantic Poetry

Mihai Eminescu

"Luceafărul"

Original: "Și ea ar iubi un zeu, dar nu-i poate înțelege zborul..."

Alphabetized: "Și ea ar iubi un zeu, dar nu-i poate înțelege zborul..."

Translation: "And she would love a god, yet cannot grasp his flight..."

"O, rămâi"

Original: "Oh, rămâi, iubirea mea, sub lumina lunii..."

Alphabetized: "Oh, rămâi, iubirea mea, sub lumina lunii..."

Translation: "Oh, stay, my love, beneath the moonlight's glow..."

"Dorința"

Original: "Inima mea este o pasăre sălbatică, dorind mâna ta..."

Alphabetized: "Inima mea este o pasăre sălbatică, dorind mâna ta..."

Translation: "My heart is a wild bird, longing for your hand..."

Man'yōshū

Poem 5

Original: "夜の深く、星々が君の名を囁く..."

Alphabetized: "Yoru no fukaku, hoshiboshi ga kimi no na o sasayaku..."

Translation: "In the deep of night, the stars whisper your name..."

Poem 127

Original: "たとえ離れていても、私の心は君に向かう..."

Alphabetized: "Tatoe hanarete itemo, watashi no kokoro wa kimi ni mukau..."

Translation: "Though miles apart, my heart travels to you..."

Hyakunin Isshu

Poem 31

Original: "今、私の流す涙は、朝の花に降る露のように..."

Alphabetized: "Ima, watashi no nagasu namida wa, asa no hana ni furu tsuyu no you ni..."

Translation: "The tears I shed now fall, like dew on morning flowers..."

Isabel Allende

Translation: "Love is a tapestry woven from the threads of dreams..."

Gabriel Garcia Márquez

From "Love in the Time of Cholera"

Original: "Era inevitable: el olor de las almendras amargas siempre le recordaba el destino del amor no correspondido..."

Translation: "It was inevitable: the scent of bitter almonds always reminded him of the fate of unrequited love..."

Andrea Chénier

Translation: "Love alone can conquer the tempestuous seas of life..."
