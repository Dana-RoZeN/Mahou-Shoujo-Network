Poetry

Mihai Eminescu

Original Romanian:

"Aș vrea să te sărut, ca'n noaptea de mai,
Cu buzele sărut, cu florile de cais."

Alphabetized Romanian:

"Aș, ca'n, cu, cu, de, de, florile, mai, sărut, să te, văd, zâmbet."

English Translation:

"I wish to kiss you, like in the night of May,
With my lips kissed, with the flowers of the apricot tree."

Man'yōshū

Original Japanese:

"君がため　春の野に出でて　わかなを採る
わが衣手に　雪は降りつつ"

Alphabetized Romanization:

"Kimi ga tame haru no yo ni idete wakana o toru
Waga koromo de ni yuki wa furitsutsu"

English Translation:

"For you, I venture into the spring fields to gather young herbs,
While snow continues to fall upon my sleeves."

Gabriel García Márquez

Original Spanish:

"El corazón tiene razones que la razón no entiende."

English Translation:

"The heart has its reasons which reason knows not."

Isabel Allende

Original Spanish:

"Te quiero más que a nada en el mundo, mi amor."

English Translation:

"I love you more than anything in the world, my love."

Andrea Chénier

Original French:

"L'amour est un poème que nous chantons ensemble."

English Translation:

"Love is a poem that we sing together."

Han Dynasty Poets

Original Chinese:

"月照花间影，君来共此生。"

Pinyin:

"Yuè zhào huā jiān yǐng, jūn lái gòng cǐ shēng."

English Translation:

"The moon illuminates the shadows among the flowers; come, let us share this life together."

Rumi

Original Persian:

"عشق مانند آتش است، از آن دوری کن و خودت بسوزی."

English Translation:

"Love is like fire; stay away, and you’ll burn yourself."
